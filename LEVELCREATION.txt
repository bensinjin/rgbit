1) Create a level in TheReds1.js or whatever "world" your building for. Ensure the levelID is correct.
2) Open routes.js add an intro route and a level route.
3) Open <your-world>LevelIntros.js, add an entry for your intro.
4) Open <your-world>LevelSelect.js, add an entry for your level button.
5) Add your level title to game-config.js
6) Add necessary Level ids to utils.js for scoring
